Goals

  • Engage student developers in real world development practices
  • Encourage the initiation and team development of open-source and proprietary projects around the university

Take A Look

SUDO is a new society at the University of Canberra, we’re focused on innovation, planning & development and actual implementation in the technology sector.

We aim to be an entity to which members can belong and feel like they’re not on their own in pursuing their projects or to connect them with existing projects.

While primarily for STEM students for obvious reasons we aim to impact the UC Community as a whole through proposed campus interactivity and society tooling projects.
